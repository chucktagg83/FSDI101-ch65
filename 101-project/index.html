Introduction

The Washington Commanders are a professional American football team based in the Washington, D.C. metropolitan area. They are a member of the National Football League (NFL) and compete in the league's National Football Conference (NFC) East division.History

The team was established in 1932 as the Boston Braves, later changing to the Boston Redskins and then moving to Washington, D.C., in 1937.

In 2022, the team rebranded as the Washington Commanders, moving away from the previous name to embrace a new identity.
